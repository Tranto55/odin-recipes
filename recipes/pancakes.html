Pancakes

A slice of heaven

Pancakes are fun, pancakes are awesome, pancakes are AMAZING!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Groundnut oil
  • Nutmegs

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of flour with butter and mix
  2. Introduce your nutmeg
  3. Heat some Groundnut oil in a pan
  4. Add water to your mix
  5. Add some salt and stir
  6. Pour your mix into the pan
  7. Let it fry till it turns brown
  8. Take it off the pan and put on a plate
  9. Fry the next batch
