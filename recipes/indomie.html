Indomie

Nigeria's Go-To guy

For real though this is the food of life. It has saved SO many lives in my country. lol

Ingredients

  • indomie Super pack
  • Carrots
  • pepper
  • Groundnut oil
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add your Indomie noodles
  3. Let it boil for 10 minutes
  4. Prepare your carrots, pepper, onions
  5. Add them into the pot
  6. Put in your seasoning
  7. Allow to boil for another 5 minuites
